Live Patient, Hands-On Training

The only effective way to truly learn a procedure is with live-patient, hands-on training. Through our various training methods, practitioners can more easily facilitate the learning process and acquire their certification in aesthetics. During the live-patient hands-on training - whether it be through live workshops or live preceptorships (one on one, private training) - you will have the opportunity to perform procedures directly on volunteer patients, Empire has pioneered this style providing hands-on instruction.

Practitioners can select the level of training they are interested in (form beginner to advanced) or may work with an account manager to create a customized program, selecting the procedures and topics they want to learn.

Prerequisite Training

We have simplified the learning process by allowing you to complete the didactic portion of the training before you arrive for the hands-on portion of the training. There are numerous advantages to this style of training, including reducing the amount of time you must spend away from your home and practice. To complete the prerequisite training, you have many flexible options.

Practitioners are provided with a professional learning platform to view all procedure videos in advance, study course materials, and complete the required online exam. Alternatively (or additionally), practitioners can choose to participate in our mobile-friendly Virtual. Live training sessions, this allows our participants to view the didactic portion of the workshop LIVE, Affording them the opportunity to interact remotely yet directly with the instructors, ask questions, make comments, etc. These virtual Courses are scheduled frequently can choose the best time that will accommodate your busy schedule.
